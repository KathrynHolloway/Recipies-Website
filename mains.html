Mains:

Cottage Pie

This cottage pie is a great family meal, simple and easy to make and all in one oven dish (less washing up!) but also great for students or people cooking for 1. It works both ways because you can either eat it all straight away or make lots of the base mix and freeze it in seperate portions for another day when you want a low effot but nutritious meal.

Chilli Con Carne

Cheap and easy to make, this chilli con carne is perfect for feeding lots of hungry mouths for a casual family meal or when you have freinds over. Or, alternatively, bulk make it and freeze several portions for a quick healthy meal when you're too busy to do proper cooking. I always make sure I have a few portions in the freezer when I have lots of deadines so there is one less thing to be stressed about!
