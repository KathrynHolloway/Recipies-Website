Soups:

Tomato Soup

A classic family favourite, cant really go wrong wiht this one! Great for warming you up from the inside out on a cold day. Best paired with fresh buttered white crusty bread or croutons. You could even bake fresh bread using my bread recipe or if you're not feeling that adventurous, most supermarkets sell part baked baguettes or rolls which work well too.

Leek and Potato Soup

Have this soup for a starter for a formal sit down meal or freeze it for another day when you want a healthy lunch without all the hassle. Enjoy with fresh baked bread, croutons, or just wack some part baked bread rolls in the oven if you're short on time.
