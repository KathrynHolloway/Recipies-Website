Sticky Bread

Ingredients:

  • 160g Sultanas
  • 80g Sugar
  • 2 Weetabix
  • 130ml Milk
  • 200g SR flour

Method:

  1. Mix together all of the dry ingredients in a large bowl. Personally, I find that crushing the weetabix even just a little really helps.
  2. Add the milk and leave to soak for roughly 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the flour and mix well.
  4. Bake in a loaf tin (about 500g in size) in the oven for 45 minutes at 180ËšC.
  5. Pierce with a skewer or knife. If the skewer/knife comes back clean then remove from the oven and leave to cool.
