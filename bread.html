Bread

Ingredients:

  • 250g Strong bread flour
  • 1 Level tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp sugar
  • 5g Yeast
  • 125ml Warm water

Method:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Add the water and mix to form a soft, not sticky, dough.
  3. Remove from the bowl and knead
    If you don't know how to knead dough, don't worry! Click the button at the bottom of the page for a helpful video.
    for 5 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a loaf tin and leave to rise for 20-30 mins.
  5. Once risen, place in the oven for 20 minutes at 220ËšC until cooked all the way through. You can check this easily by pushing a knife of skewer into the centre. If it comes out clean then it's done.
