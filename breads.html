Breads:

Sticky Bread

Super quick, easy and cheap to make. This bread is great sliced with a little butter as a snack for on the go or with a packed lunch.

Banana Loaf

This sweet bread tastes great on its own or with butter and makes a good quick snack for when you're peckish but haven't got time to make something. Perfect for when you want a change from your usual fruit, cereal bars or crisps.

Bread

There's nothing nicer than fresh, still hot out of the oven, homemade bread with a bit of butter melting on top. Or, if you're feeling a little adventurous, bake a small wheel of camembert cheese in the oven at the same time as your bread and use it for dipping, tastes amazing!
